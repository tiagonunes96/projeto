Floppy Disk - Rethinking the old

UX/UI Design

The digital product named as FloppyDisk arises as solution for the challege proposed at User Interface subject. This creative, revival and playful idea was thought to be a product focussed to enhance the ubiquitous Floppy Disk and raise the value as a rare piece plus unique collector item. In the end we had been challeged to create an e-commerce platform to sell the product in study.
